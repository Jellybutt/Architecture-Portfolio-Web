♥ Public Restroom with A GreenHouse in Jackson Park ♥





In this project, as a nature lover, I chose to build a sustainable and eco-friendly
restroom in the community farm in Jackson Park. Everything in here is enjoyable and fresh. Costumers and visitors are able to relax their soul here. Back to nature!
