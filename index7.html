♣ Tea House ♣








This model is built from the below image which based on my free imagination. This project is another project that focuses on exploring the nature. There are six individual pavilions in this project, and you can imagine the shape of one single pavilion is like the shape of water drop, a petal or a piece of leaf. If you see these six individuals as a whole, they can form a shape of flower or a group of lotus leaves on the pond.
